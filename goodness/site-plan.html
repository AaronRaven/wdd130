Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to have a comfy LDS friendly website . It will feature uplifting and comfy quotes and pictures. Please notice that the colours of the website are based on classic teddy bear. It does look calming. Ironically the colours are same as latte coffe.

Audience

The website is LDS friendly, but not LDS exclusive thought-the intended audience are people who want comfy and uplifting content.

Content

Home page

Welcome to goodness.com! A website dedicated to good vibes. Here you can find inspirational quotes, and uplifting pictures. Every day you can find tailor made content just for you. Everything and anything to make your day at least a little bit better. Please click here to go to picture section of the website. Please click here to go to the quote section of the website.

Images for the Home page

Pictures

“A picture is worth a thousand words” Welcome to our page dedicated to inspirational pictures. Here every three days you can find selected uplifting pictures ranging from science (like space rockets) to spirituality(like old European Churches or LDS temples). From nature to architecture. From stuffed animals to cup pictures. All the pictures are non-copyrighter. Users are more than welcome to print them and take inspiration for personalised pillows, cups or even phone cases.

Images for the Page 2/The picture page

Quotes

"For many of us, inspiration is triggered by the quips and quotations of others." Emilee Day, The Complete Book of Inspirational Quotations. Welcome to our page dedicated to inspirational quotes. Here every three days you can find selected inspirational quotes ranging from science to spirituality. From books and movies. From Church and community leaders to politicians and inspirational figures. All the quotes here are non-copyrighter. Users are welcome to save them, or even print them as they like.

Wireframes

Home

A home page will feature desciption of the page. Bellow the descirption, there will be two pictures with links to the other two pages. The links will also be found on the header and footer.

Quotes

A quote page will most likely be a flex(flex is a bit different than what I thought it was, so I used grid). The page will feature desciption of the page, and bellow it inspirational quotes.It will feature quotes in writen and/or picture format. A flex will be just a one line, hence the flex, and no the grid.The header and footer will be the same on all three pages.

Pictures

A picture page will feature a 3x2 (original plan was2x3) grid with pictures. On top of the page there will be the desciption of the page. Bellow the desciption, there will be the afermentioned grid of with six pictures. The header and footer will be the same on all three pages.
